Progress

进度条组件， .fn-progress 为容器， .fn-progress-bar 为进度显示信息。

基本样式

40%
						
<div class="fn-progress">
  <div class="fn-progress-bar" style="width: 30%"></div>
</div>

<div class="fn-progress">
  <div class="fn-progress-bar" style="width: 40%">40%</div>
</div>

进度条颜色

进度条默认为全局主色，在进度条上添加相应的类可设置的颜色：

  • .fn-progress-bar-secondary
  • .fn-progress-bar-success
  • .fn-progress-bar-warning
  • .fn-progress-bar-danger
						
<div class="fn-progress">
  <div class="fn-progress-bar" style="width: 15%"></div>
</div>

<div class="fn-progress">
  <div class="fn-progress-bar fn-progress-bar-secondary" style="width: 30%"></div>
</div>

<div class="fn-progress">
  <div class="fn-progress-bar fn-progress-bar-success" style="width: 45%"></div>
</div>

<div class="fn-progress">
  <div class="fn-progress-bar fn-progress-bar-warning" style="width: 60%"></div>
</div>

<div class="fn-progress">
  <div class="fn-progress-bar fn-progress-bar-danger" style="width: 75%"></div>
</div>

进度条高度

在 .fn-progress 添加 .fn-progress-xs .fn-progress-sm 可以设置进度条高度。 

						
<div class="fn-progress fn-progress-xs">
    <div class="fn-progress-bar" style="width: 80%"></div>
</div>

<div class="fn-progress fn-progress-sm">
    <div class="fn-progress-bar" style="width: 60%"></div>
</div>

<div class="fn-progress">
    <div class="fn-progress-bar" style="width: 40%"></div>
</div>

进度条条纹

在进度条容器上添加 .fn-progress-striped 显示条纹效果，可结合进度条颜色 class 使用。 

						
<div class="fn-progress fn-progress-striped">
  <div class="fn-progress-bar fn-progress-bar-danger" style="width: 80%"></div>
</div>
<div class="fn-progress fn-progress-striped">
  <div class="fn-progress-bar fn-progress-bar-warning" style="width: 60%"></div>
</div>
<div class="fn-progress fn-progress-striped">
  <div class="fn-progress-bar fn-progress-bar-success" style="width: 45%"></div>
</div>
<div class="fn-progress fn-progress-striped">
  <div class="fn-progress-bar fn-progress-bar-secondary" style="width: 30%"></div>
</div>

<div class="fn-progress fn-progress-striped">
  <div class="fn-progress-bar" style="width: 15%"></div>
</div>

进度条动画

进度条容器上添加 .fn-active 激活进度条动画（CSS Animation）。 

						
<div class="fn-progress fn-progress-striped fn-progress-sm fn-active ">
  <div class="fn-progress-bar fn-progress-bar-secondary"  style="width: 57%"></div>
</div>

进度条堆叠

Male
Female
Other
						
<div class="fn-progress">
  <div class="fn-progress-bar"  style="width: 65%">Male</div>
  <div class="fn-progress-bar fn-progress-bar-success"  style="width: 15%">Female</div>
  <div class="fn-progress-bar fn-progress-bar-warning"  style="width: 20%">Other</div>
</div>
